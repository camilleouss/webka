How was the Kazakh brand Qazaq Republic created?

The founder of the brand Saken Zholdas - about how his passion for design began, how their brand is unique and why it is important to promote the modern identity of Kazakhstan. About Qazaq Republic

We launched in early 2017. We did not have confidence in the quality of the products, so for the first year we worked in test mode.

The very first point of sale was Duty Free in Almaty. Management liked the presentation and packaging so much products, that they immediately bought out half of the stocks at that time. I didn’t think it would go so well, so don’t began to invest a lot in the project. The initial investment was $ 10,000.

Now we have more than 30 points throughout Kazakhstan. We work with the country's top retailers, such as Meloman, Duty Free, Shymbulak, FashionPark, Gazpromneft. During this time, stocks have grown almost 20 times.

Some people think that we only sell T-shirts, but in fact we already have 40 items goods. We are developing several directions: not only a clothing line, but also Drinkware. For example, we recently introduced series thermal bottles "Bótelke", which became a hit of sales. In the fall, we launched the Sómke line of bags and they also entered the market well. In general, all of our products sell well, which cannot but motivate.

In the future, we plan to introduce more assortment. But things are not as simple as they seem. Introduction new a product is a whole process of several parts. For example, T-shirts come to us from a factory that work with big brands such as Uniqlo, Levi's. Then a print is applied separately in the workshop. Packages, lids and labels for tubes are produced in different factories. As a result, all this is collected into one product on our website. warehouse.

"The idea of our brand is to convey a piece of culture, national identity. The uniqueness of our brand is in high-quality presentation and modern style, which is not a shame to wear."

We would rather work with manufacturers who have experience, and focus on the design of prints. AND this is only the logistics part, and there is also the RnD part when we are looking for packaging solutions, creating designs for labels and prints. The process is laborious, but interesting and gives me the opportunity to grow, v first of all, as a designer. But most importantly, we see and feel support among the population and the brand on the sly becomes recognizable. This year we focused more on offline sales, but soon we will launch online store, which will make it easy to shop, as well as expand the geographic scope of products.

Now there are many brands on the market with a national presentation and an emphasis on ornaments. We took a different path. Our goal - to promote the new identity of the country both inside and outside its borders. Therefore, we write phrases in English. language.

By the way, many people think that the word Qazaq is about the nation, but in fact, as Yerlan Karin noted, the word Qazaq means “free” from ancient Türkic, that's why our prints have a double meaning. This is a play on words: Born to be Qazaq / Born to be Free. Many foreigners order this particular print, send their photos from our sweatshirts or T-shirts. Therefore, I think that through our prints we also promote the country's brand.
